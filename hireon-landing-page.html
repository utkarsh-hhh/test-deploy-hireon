How It Works

From application to offer,
all automated.

Four intelligent phases that take a candidate from resume submission to hiring decision — with AI doing the heavy lifting at every step.

Phase 01 — Intake
AI Resume Intelligence

The moment a candidate uploads their resume, Hireon's AI engine kicks in. It parses the document, extracts explicit skills like React, Node.js and TypeScript, and infers hidden skills like Redux, REST APIs, and Docker from context clues. Experience years are calculated precisely and seniority level is determined automatically.

Skill Extraction Experience Calc Seniority Detection ~10 seconds
🧠
Phase 02 — Scoring
Smart Auto-Shortlisting

Hireon compares each candidate's profile against your requirements and generates a precise match score. If a candidate clears your threshold (e.g. 80%), they're instantly shortlisted, their status is updated, your HR team receives a dashboard notification, and the candidate gets an automated email — all without a single human action.

Match Scoring Auto-Shortlist HR Notification Fully Automated
🎯
Phase 03 — Scheduling
Conflict-Free Scheduling

One click. Hireon cross-references the candidate's availability, the interviewer's calendar, and checks for existing bookings. It selects the optimal slot, generates a Google Meet link, and dispatches calendar invites to everyone involved. What used to take 15 emails and 3 days now takes 30 seconds.

Slot Matching Conflict Detection Meet Link 30 seconds
📅
Phase 04 — Decision
Interview Intelligence & Hiring

Post-interview, the interviewer submits structured feedback. Hireon's AI analyzes it, generates a summary, updates the hire probability score (e.g. 75% → 82%), and surfaces a hiring recommendation to HR. Every decision is data-backed. Every candidate — hired or not — is stored permanently in your searchable talent database for future roles.

Feedback Analysis Hire Probability Talent Database AI Recommendation

FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started with Hireon.

How accurate is the AI match scoring?
+
Our AI achieves an average of 87% match accuracy across all roles. It's trained on millions of resume-to-hire outcomes and continuously improves from your team's feedback and hiring decisions.
What resume formats does Hireon support?
+
Hireon processes PDF, DOCX, and plain text resumes. We handle everything from standard templates to custom-designed CVs and LinkedIn exports. Parsing typically completes in under 10 seconds.
How does the scheduling work exactly?
+
Candidates submit 3–5 available time windows. Hireon cross-references these against your interviewers' calendars (via Google Calendar or Outlook), picks the optimal slot, generates a Meet link, and sends invites to everyone. One click, done.
Is candidate data stored securely?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're GDPR and SOC 2 compliant. Candidates can request data deletion at any time. Enterprise plans include custom data retention policies.
Can I use Hireon with my existing ATS?
+
Hireon integrates with major ATS platforms including Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and BambooHR on Enterprise plans. Starter and Growth plans can use our API or Zapier integration for custom workflows.
What happens to rejected candidates?
+
All candidates are permanently stored in your Talent Database — fully indexed and searchable. When a new role opens that matches a previous candidate's profile, Hireon automatically alerts you. Many customers hire from this pool within months.
